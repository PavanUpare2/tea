Types of Tea

  • Green Tea - Light and fresh with grassy notes.
  • Black Tea - Bold and robust, great for mornings.
  • Oolong Tea - Somewhere between green and black, complex flavor.
  • Herbal Tea - Caffeine-free infusions of herbs and flowers.
  • White Tea - Subtle and delicate flavor, minimally processed.

Brewing Tips

  1. Use fresh, cold water.
  2. Heat water to the right temperature for your tea type.
  3. Steep for the recommended time.
  4. Enjoy without rushing!
